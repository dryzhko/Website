About

I'm a Computer Science student, who's also into making music. As a student, I'm interested in all different kinds of development, and am eager to learn new and exciting technologies. This summer, I interned at the music streaming service Pandora as a software engineering intern, and got to work on some interesting real world projects. I will be returning as a fulltime software engineer after I graduate in December 2017. Take a look at my resume section to learn more about my experience.

In my free time, I like to make music, check some of it out here! I like to experiment with different genres and techniques in order to come up with a unique sound. If you're into experimental, ambient, downtempo, trippy music give it a listen!
